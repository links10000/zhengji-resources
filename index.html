Zhengji Windows & Doors

Official website: https://zhengjiwindows.com/

Zhengji Windows & Doors is a China-based manufacturer providing one-stop custom processing for aluminum windows and doors. With 21 years of manufacturing experience, we support project-based orders for North America buyers with stable quality control and reliable delivery schedules.

What we offer

  • One-stop custom processing: mold making, extrusion, surface finishing, CNC machining, welding/assembly, QC.
  • Seamless welding: cleaner appearance and consistent joints for premium projects.
  • Customization support: free sampling support, MOQ 500 kg, custom packaging options.

Typical custom flow

  1. CAD drawing submission
  2. Drawing analysis & confirmation
  3. Mold manufacturing
  4. Sample production
  5. Sample testing & approval
  6. Mass production
  7. Packing & shipping
  8. After-sales support
